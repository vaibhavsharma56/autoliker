What is an Access Token?

Access token is a code that our application will use to get access to your facebook account and give you likes.

Why Should I allow Followers?

Its because this application will work in public, public can be set when you are 18+, and so if you allow followers its obvious that you are 18+.

Why Post/Status ID's are needed?

Post/Status/Photo ID's are the Unique IDs given specific to each by facebook, so we need your ID's to give likes on them.

Do my ID get Block by using Get Like Fast?

How To get Post ID?

Open the Status in a tab and your URL will be something like this - 'https://www.facebook.com/username/posts/347513781973974' The Red Color Part There is your post/status ID In Photo Or Video Post - 'https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=6456243725134313&set=a.151357394922948.31228.100001460669189' The Color part you see is your ID.

How To get My Profile ID?

Open your Cover Pic or any pic you uploaded on facebook, then the URL will be - 'https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=308892772553357&set=a.143893082386661.28026.100002979104441&type=1' in this the Colour part is your Profile ID.
